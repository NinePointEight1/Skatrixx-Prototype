Game Of Skate
One player sets a trick by doing a particular skateboarding trick of their choice. If the trick is not landed, or the board did not fully rotate another player attempts to set a trick. If the player lands the trick they have gained the lead and set the trick.
60 min
2-5
Trick, Skate, Kickflip, Game
Follow The Leader
To play, designate one person as the leader. This person will do a skate move (spinning, jumping, dancing, etc.) and all of the other players must copy them as closely as possible. If you can't reproduce the leader's moves, you're out!
20 min
2-5
Trick, Leader, Short, Ollie, Game
Cops & Robbers
Divide in 2 groups and set up 10-20 cones randomly throughout the skatepark. The robbers skate around knocking over the cones while the cops skate around putting them back upright. After 10 minutes count the cones and see which team has more cones in their favour, winning the game.
10 min
4-10
Agiity, Short, Groups
Obstacle Course
Set up cones, chairs, other skateboards, around the skatepark and be as creative as possible jumping over them. Try to get as many consecutive obstacle tricks as possible.
20 min
1-2
Technical, Obstacles, Tricks
